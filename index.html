About Us

At Brew Haven, we believe coffee is more than just a drink- it's an experience. From handpicked beans and rich blends to soothing teas and refreshing matcha, every cup is made with care and passion. Whether you’re here to work, catch up with friends, or simply enjoy a quiet moment, our space is designed to make you feel at home. Join us for a sip, a smile, and a little escape from the everyday.

Our Menu

Cappuccino

A timeless Italian favorite — one shot of espresso blended with steamed milk and topped with a layer of silky foam.

Matcha Latte

Bright and energizing — made with premium Japanese matcha whisked into creamy steamed milk.

Mocha

A rich mix of bold espresso, velvety milk, and smooth chocolate, finished with a touch of sweetness.

Flat White

Originating from Australia & New Zealand — a double shot of espresso topped with micro-foam for a strong yet smooth flavor.

Caramel Macchiato

Espresso poured over warm milk with vanilla syrup, finished with a golden drizzle of caramel. Sweet and indulgent.

Cold Brew

Slow-steeped in cool water for hours, this smooth and refreshing coffee has a natural sweetness and less acidity.

Americano

A bold shot of espresso diluted with hot water — smooth, simple, and perfect for coffee purists.

Iced Latte

Chilled espresso poured over cold milk and ice, creating a refreshing, creamy pick-me-up.
